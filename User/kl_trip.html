KUALA LUMPUR FULL DAY TOUR



Kuala Lumpur has managed to retain her charm, with a skyline that blends the Old World with the New. See the contrast of the distinctive Indo-Moorish architecture and the ultra modern edifices, Petronas Twin Towers - tallest in the world. Beside this, you will visit to Malaysia King's Palace, National Monument and a lot more. This full day tours in including Batu Caves, the most famous cave landmark in Kuala Lumpur, which also doubles up as a religious side for Hindus. Ascend 272 steps to reach the top where you can admire the limestone formations and watch devotees pray to their shrines.
